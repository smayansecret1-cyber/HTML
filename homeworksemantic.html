kokoshibo.

⚔️ Who he is: Upper Moon One — the strongest of Muzan’s Twelve Kizuki.

👦 Past Life: Born as Michikatsu Tsugikuni, twin brother of Yoriichi (the first Sun Breather).

😈 Why he became a demon: Couldn’t accept being weaker than Yoriichi and feared aging and death.

🌙 Breathing Style: Moon Breathing — derived from Sun Breathing, creates crescent-shaped slashes.

💀 Appearance: Six eyes, long dark hair, and a sword fused with his flesh.

💪 Abilities: Immense strength, speed, and regeneration; powerful swordsmanship and Blood Demon Art.

🧠 Personality: Proud, jealous, disciplined, but deeply insecure about his brother’s strength.
