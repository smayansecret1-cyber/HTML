What is JJK?

Jujutsu Kaisen (often called JJK) is a popular action and supernatural anime created by Gege Akutami. The story follows Yuji Itadori, a high school student who becomes involved in the dangerous world of curses after swallowing a powerful cursed object — one of Sukuna’s fingers. He then joins Tokyo Jujutsu High, a school where sorcerers are trained to fight cursed spirits that are born from negative human emotions. The series is known for its intense fight scenes, dark themes, strong characters like Gojo Satoru and Ryomen Sukuna, and high-quality animation. Jujutsu Kaisen has become one of the most popular new-generation anime worldwide.
